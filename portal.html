How It Works

Travel For Growth is designed to help you get more from your travel — satisfying experiences, personal growth, and trips that align with who you are. We aim to help you choose based on who you are, not on which destinations have the biggest marketing budgets.

📋 The Travel For Growth (TFG) Assessment

A 40-item personality test reveals your approach to travel. TFG places people on a spectrum from relaxation/recharge travelers to transformation travelers that look to learn insights about themselves and the world around them. In between are people who focus on adventure, the cultures of the world, and their own sense of inner understanding.

The TFG also asks questions that are helpful for trip planning — your travel passions, your life stage and travel style. If you have a regular travel partner, we can help you improve your working relationship. If you work with a travel partner, you can both take the TFG and double the benefits.

🧬 DNA Guide

Our personalized insight on a destination that interests you. We have 3,500 locations in our database. If we don't have it, just enter a place and TbyT will create a DNA Guide for you. You get 5 DNA Guides for free and can purchase more or unlock them with a Subscription.

✈️ Trips

Trips are either journeys you have already taken or something you are planning for the future — next week or three years from now.

🗺️ Places

A Place is somewhere you go and stay as part of a trip. A trip can be entirely in one place or span 25 different ones. We provide a map that shows how the Places relate to each other geographically.

📖 Your Trip Story

A script of your travel life. It contains your Archetype and Dimension story from your Assessment, and shows trips you have taken and are planning to take. If this is filled in, our application can use this historical view to suggest future trips and destinations with those places that you like the most.

👥 Workshops

We offer two two-hour workshops to help you achieve personal growth. Our Pre-Trip Workshop helps you understand your TFG Assessment and prepares you for a great trip experience. Our Integration Workshop takes place after a trip — it ensures that your most important learnings get folded into your daily life. Both of these workshops are delivered on demand — you can watch as much as you want.

💬 Trip Support

Daily encouragement and a daily invitation to journal your reflections while on your trip. This support continues for six months after a trip is completed (or until you want it to stop).

⭐ Subscription

Available as monthly or annual plans — choose whichever works best for you. A Subscription unlocks workshops, trip planning, and additional DNA Guides.
