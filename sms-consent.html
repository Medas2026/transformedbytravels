Daily Journal SMS Reminders

During your trip, Transformed by Travels sends you one SMS per day to prompt your travel reflection journal. You opt in when setting up your trip journal — here is exactly what that looks like.

Program Details
  • Message frequency: 1 SMS per day, only during active trip dates
  • Sender: Transformed by Travels (TravelForGrowth)
  • To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message
  • To get help, reply HELP or email support@transformedbytravels.com
  • Msg & data rates may apply
  • Supported carriers: AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and most US carriers
