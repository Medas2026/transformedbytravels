For Serious Birders

Connected to the world's largest birding network.

For dedicated birders, the platform integrates directly with eBird — the Cornell Lab of Ornithology's global database of 1.5 billion bird sightings. Before the trip, travelers get a target species list drawn from real eBird data at Uganda's top hotspots. In the field, notable recent sightings tell them what other birders are seeing right now.

🎯
Pre-Trip Target List
Before departure, the platform pulls the complete species list from the highest-rated eBird hotspot near each park on the itinerary. Filtered by travel month, so travelers know exactly which species are realistic targets — not just historically recorded.
📡
Live Notable Sightings
eBird's real-time notable sightings API surfaces rare and unusual species reported by other birders within 50km — updated daily. Traveler's guide knows what's being seen before the morning drive.
📋
Trip Life List
Every species ticked in the field app accumulates into a personal life list that spans trips and years. Serious birders maintain life lists obsessively — this becomes their canonical record, tied to Uganda.
Journey Book Integration
The birding trip culminates in a printed Journey Book — a narrative memoir with photos, maps, and the complete species log. A keepsake no birder would discard, and a natural conversation starter that drives referrals.

A Partnership for Uganda

Built with Uganda Wildlife Authority.

The platform is designed from the ground up to serve Uganda's conservation mission — not just traveler convenience. We are actively seeking a data partnership with Uganda Wildlife Authority to make this the most authoritative wildlife tracking platform in East Africa.

What UWA Provides
→
Licensed professional wildlife photography from Uganda's parks
→
Authoritative species lists validated by park rangers
→
Ranger field intelligence on animal movements and activity
→
Credibility: "In partnership with Uganda Wildlife Authority"
What UWA Receives
→
GPS-precise sighting data from thousands of traveler trips — aggregated and anonymised
→
A citizen science network across all major parks at zero cost
→
Monthly sighting intelligence reports by park and species
→
Traveler data that disappears today is captured and returned to Uganda

Why It Matters for Uganda

A platform that works for travelers and for Uganda.

Every interaction creates value at two levels — a richer experience for the traveler, and a growing intelligence asset for Uganda's wildlife conservation and tourism ecosystem.

🗺️
Proprietary Wildlife Intelligence
GPS-precise sighting data — aggregated across thousands of traveler trips — builds Uganda's most detailed real-world map of where species are seen, when, and in what numbers. No research institution has this at traveler scale.
🔁
Repeat Visitation Driver
A personal life list is the single strongest driver of repeat safari visits. Travelers who start a life list in Uganda have a compelling reason to return — more parks, more species, more Uganda.
📚
Uganda-First Database
Built around Uganda's parks and species from day one. Not a generic global app — a platform where Uganda is the hero, curated for the traveler who chose Uganda specifically.
